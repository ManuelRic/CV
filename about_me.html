I spent ten years studying in Panama at the Metropolitan School of Panama (MET), where I began the IB program. Later, I moved back to Spain to complete the program at SEK Levante.

I’m currently studying Technology and Multimedia at the Polytechnic University of Valencia (UPV) and completing a semester abroad through ERASMUS at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU).

My interests lie in full-stack and software development, game creation (including VR), and 2D/3D design — areas where I can combine creativity and technology.

Outside academics, I enjoy outdoor activities like rock climbing, kayaking, and rugby, where I served as team captain during the club’s early years. I’ve always been drawn to art — starting with hands-on forms and later digital — and that mix of creativity and logic naturally led me to this field.
